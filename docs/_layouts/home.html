How Rosie Works

Rosie's Four Elements

Patient Data Use Agreement (PDUA)

A legal agreement between you and the patient data manager that gives you sole ownership rights of your health data and doest not allow the patient data manager to use, share, or sell your data without your explicit consent.

Patient Data Manager (PDM)

A service that acquires, aggregates, curates, shares and manages your patient's health data on your behalf.

Standard Patient Health Record (SPHR)

All data that is relevant to your health available to you in a standard and computable way.

Patient Data Receipt

A fully documented and standard receipt generated at the end of each and every healthcare transaction and made available to you or your patient data manager.

Why is Rosie Free?

A not-for-profit organization operating in the public interest, MITRE envisions a healthcare system designed around and with the patient, where individuals, families, and communities have the right data, tools, and resources to improve their health literacy and maximize health and well-being.

Rosie is a key component in realizing this vision.

To maximize availability and impact, Rosie is being developed as an open source project.
